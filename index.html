About Me

I am a PhD student at IIIT Delhi (since 2021), advised by Dr. Ranjitha Prasad. My research focuses on Federated Learning, particularly:

  • Personalized Federated Learning
  • Continual Federated Learning
  • Unsupervised & Decentralized Federated Learning

I am a recipient of the Prime Minister’s Fellowship for Doctoral Research (SERB–FICCI). Currently, I am a Visiting Scholar at Wayne State University, Detroit, working with Dr. Prashant Khanduri.

Previously, I worked at ISI Kolkata (DRDO project on Hardware Trojan Detection using X-ray imaging) and at IDRBT Hyderabad on reversible data hiding in images. I completed my 5-year Integrated M.Tech in Computer Science from the University of Hyderabad.
